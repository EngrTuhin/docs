# Welcome to Koorri Sales & Inventory System

Koorri Sales & Inventory System helps you manage your daily business work from one place.

You can use this system to manage products, sales, purchases, stock, customers, suppliers, payments, and reports.

<Info>
This guide is written for regular users. You do not need technical knowledge to follow it.
</Info>

---

# What Can You Do in This System?

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Sell Products" icon="shopping-cart">
    Create sales invoices, receive payments, manage deliveries, and handle product returns.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Manage Stock" icon="package">
    Check current stock, add opening stock, transfer stock, adjust stock, and record damaged products.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Buy Products" icon="truck">
    Create purchase orders, record purchase invoices, manage supplier payments, and purchase returns.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Manage Products" icon="box">
    Add products, categories, units, brands, sizes, colors, models, shelves, and barcodes.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Manage Customers" icon="users">
    Add customers, customer branches, sales persons, and customer payment records.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Manage Suppliers" icon="building">
    Add suppliers, track purchase records, and manage supplier payments.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Manage Finance" icon="wallet">
    Record income, expenses, bank accounts, mobile banking, loans, investments, and withdrawals.
  </Card>

  <Card title="View Reports" icon="bar-chart">
    Check sales reports, purchase reports, stock reports, due reports, and financial reports.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

# Main Menu Guide

You will find all features in the left sidebar menu.

| Menu | Simple Meaning |
|---|---|
| Dashboard | See business summary |
| Product Configuration | Set up products before sales or purchase |
| Sales Management | Create sales invoices, delivery, returns, and collections |
| Business Contacts | Manage customers, suppliers, and sales persons |
| Purchase Management | Manage purchases and supplier payments |
| Inventory Management | Manage stock and warehouses |
| Company Purchase Order | Manage customer/company purchase orders |
| Quotation Management | Create quotations for customers |
| Work Order Information | Manage work orders |
| Finance | Manage expenses, banks, loans, and investments |
| Reports & Analytics | View business reports |
| Settings | Manage users, roles, permissions, and system settings |

# Best Way to Start

If you are using the system for the first time, follow this order.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set Up Products">
    First, add product categories, units, brands, sizes, colors, and products.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Customers and Suppliers">
    Add the people and companies you sell to or buy from.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Up Warehouse or Shop">
    Create the warehouse or shop where your products are stored.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Opening Stock">
    Enter your current stock before starting sales or purchase.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start Sales and Purchase">
    Create sales invoices, purchase invoices, payments, and returns.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check Reports">
    Review stock, sales, purchase, due, and finance reports.
  </Step>
</Steps>

# User Permission Note

<Warning>
You may not see all menus.
</Warning>

Your menu depends on your user role and permission.

For example:

| User Type | May See |
|---|---|
| Admin | All menus |
| Sales User | Sales and customer menus |
| Inventory User | Product and stock menus |
| Accounts User | Finance and reports menus |

If you cannot find a menu, please contact your system administrator.

# Before You Start

Make sure these basic items are ready:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Product Information">
    Product name, category, unit, brand, sales price, purchase price, and barcode if needed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Customer Information">
    Customer name, phone number, address, and branch information if needed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Supplier Information">
    Supplier name, phone number, address, and opening balance if needed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Stock Information">
    Warehouse/shop name and current opening stock quantity.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
