Use this page when you need to:

- Check previous sales

- Print invoice again
- Check today’s sales summary
- Check monthly sales summary
- Check due amount
- Check discount and grand total
- Manage sales from the action menu

---

# Open All Sales Records

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Sales Management">
    From the left sidebar, go to **Sales Management**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click All Sales Records">
    Click **All Sales Records**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Sales List">
    The system will show all saved sales invoices.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

# Quick Overview

At the top of the page, you can see a quick sales summary.

| Summary Box | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Today Sales | Total number of sales today |
| Today Amount | Total sales amount today |
| Today Collected | Total collected amount today |
| Today Due | Total due amount today |
| Today Avg Ticket | Average invoice amount today |
| Collection Rate | Collection performance |
| Month Sales | Total sales in this month |
| Month Amount | Total sales amount in this month |
| Month Collected | Total collected amount in this month |
| Month Due | Total due amount in this month |
| Month Avg Ticket | Average invoice amount this month |
| Due vs Amount | Due comparison with sales amount |

<Info>
This overview helps managers quickly understand sales, collection, and due status.
</Info>

# Sales Records Table

The sales table shows invoice-wise sales information.

| Column | Meaning |
|---|---|
| SL | Serial number |
| Date | Sales date and time |
| Invoice | Invoice number |
| Customer | Customer name |
| Invoice Due | Due amount for the invoice |
| Lines | Number of items in the invoice |
| Total | Invoice total before discount |
| Discount Total | Total discount amount |
| Grand Total | Final invoice amount |
| Action | View, print, return, or manage invoice |

---

# Search Sales Invoice

Use the search box to find a sales invoice quickly.

You can search by:

- Invoice number
- Customer name
- Date
- Amount if supported

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Search Box">
    Click the search box at the top-right side.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Type Search Text">
    Type invoice number, customer name, or related keyword.
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Result">
    The table will show matching sales records.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

# Filter Sales Records

Use **Filters** to find specific sales records.

You may filter by:

- Date range
- Customer
- Payment status
- Invoice type
- Due status
- Sales person if available

<Info>
Filter options may be different based on your system setup and permission.
</Info>

---

# Analytics

Click **Analytics** to view sales analysis if available.

Analytics may help you understand:

- Sales performance
- Collection status
- Due amount
- Daily or monthly sales trend
- Customer-wise sales

---

# View Invoice Details

Each sales row has an **Action** menu on the right side.

Use the action menu to manage that invoice.

Common actions may include:

| Action | Use |
|---|---|
| View | See invoice details |
| Print | Print invoice again |
| Edit | Edit invoice if permission allows |
| Return | Create return from invoice |
| Delete | Delete invoice if permission allows |

<Warning>
Edit or delete sales invoices carefully. It may affect stock, customer due, payment, and reports.
</Warning>


---

# Common Use Cases

| Need | What to Do |
|---|---|
| Find old invoice | Search by invoice number |
| Reprint customer bill | Open Action menu and print |
| Check today’s sales | See Quick Overview |
| Check monthly sales | See monthly summary boxes |
| Check due invoices | Look at Invoice Due column or use filter |
| Return sold product | Use Action menu or Sales Return List |
| Check invoice amount | See Total and Grand Total columns |

---

# Important Notes

<Info>
All completed POS invoices will appear in All Sales Records.
</Info>

<Warning>
If an invoice has due amount, collect it from **Customer Due Collection** or from the related payment option if available.
</Warning>

# Best Practice

- Check today’s sales at the end of every day
- Use search to find invoices quickly
- Use filters for date-wise reports
- Reprint invoice only when needed
- Check due invoices regularly
- Do not edit/delete invoices without permission
- Use reports for final business analysis
