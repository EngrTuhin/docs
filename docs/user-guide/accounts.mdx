# Finance & Accounts Modules

The Finance and Accounts module tracks all cash inflows and outflows, manages bank/mobile wallets, handles business loans, and records capital investments or partner withdrawals.

---

## 1. Income & Expenses

### Overview
* **Expense Categories**: Groups expenses for reporting (e.g. Rent, Electricity, Stationery).
* **Transactions (Expense Entry)**: Logs daily business payments.

### Who Can Access
* Accountants
* Branch Managers (petty cash limits)
* Administrators

### Navigation
* **Sidebar**: Finance → Income & Expense → **Transactions** or **Categories**

### Field Descriptions (Expense Entry)

| Field | Description | Rules / Validations |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Category** | Expense classification. | Select from Expense Categories list. |
| **Paid From** | Account funding the expense. | Must be Cash-in-Hand, Bank, or Mobile Wallet. |
| **Amount** | Financial cost. | Cannot exceed the selected account's balance. |
| **Reference No** | Receipt or voucher number. | Optional; useful for audit tracking. |

### Step-by-Step Instructions:
1. Navigate to **Transactions** (under Income & Expense) and click **Create Expense**.
2. Select the **Expense Category**.
3. Select the payment account under **Paid From** (e.g., Office Cash or Brac Bank).
4. Enter the **Amount** and **Transaction Date**.
5. Upload a scan of the payment receipt if required.
6. Click **Save Expense**. The cash or bank account balance will decrease by this amount.

## 2. Banking & Mobile Banking Channel Configuration

### Overview
Set up and monitor financial bank accounts and mobile money accounts (e.g. bKash, Rocket, Nagad). All POS card transactions and supplier due payments route through these configured accounts.

### Navigation
* **Sidebar**: Finance → Banking → **Bank Accounts** or **Mobile Banking**

### Step-by-Step Instructions:
1. Navigate to **Bank Accounts** and click **Add Bank**.
2. Enter the Bank Name, Account Name, Account Number, Branch, and Initial Opening Balance.
3. Click **Save**.
4. To track mobile wallets, go to **Mobile Banking**, click **Add Mobile Account**, enter the service provider, wallet number, and opening balance, and click **Save**.

## 3. Loans & Capital Setup

### Overview
* **Loan Providers**: Financial institutions or private lenders.
* **Loan Types**: Categorize loans (e.g., term loans, lines of credit).
* **Loan Taken Entry & Repayment**: Track loan disbursement and pay principal/interest installments.
* **Investment & Withdrawal**: Track capital injected into or withdrawn from the business by shareholders.

### Navigation
* **Sidebar**: Finance → Loan Setup → **Loan Taken Entry** / **Investment** / **Withdrawal**

### Step-by-Step Instructions (Repaying a Loan):
1. Navigate to **Loan Taken Entry**.
2. Locate the active loan from the list and click **Repay**.
3. In the repayment modal, enter:
   * **Repayment Amount**: The installment amount.
   * **Interest Paid**: (Optional) Interest component of the payment.
   * **Paid From**: The bank or cash account from which funds are drawn.
4. Click **Submit Repayment**. This updates the remaining loan balance and logs the cash outflow.

---

## Business Rules

* **Overdraft Restrictions**: Accounts cannot have negative balances unless an overdraft limit is explicitly configured. If an account has $100, you cannot enter a $150 expense from that account.
* **Double Entry**: Every expense or loan repayment requires selecting a valid source account. The system automatically creates credit and debit ledgers.

## Business Example

The Finance Manager needs to pay the monthly shop rent. 
1. The manager goes to **Transactions** (under Income & Expense).
2. Selects the category "Shop Rent".
3. Selects the payment account "Dutch Bangla Bank".
4. Enters the amount "$1,200".
5. Attaches the rent receipt image and saves.
DBBL Bank balance drops by $1,200, and the monthly Expense report shows Rent at $1,200.

## FAQs

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I transfer funds from Cash-in-Hand to a Bank Account?">
    You can log a fund transfer by using the Bank Deposit flow. Go to Bank Accounts, click Deposit, select Cash-in-Hand as the source, enter the amount, select the destination bank, and save.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where do I see shareholder investments?">
    Go to Finance → Loan Setup → **Investment**. This panel lists all capital injections, the date of deposit, and the funding source.
  </Accordion>
