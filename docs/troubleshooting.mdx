# Troubleshooting Guide

This guide details resolutions for common operational issues that users face when interacting with the system.

---

## 1. POS Checkout Issues

### Problem: Cashier cannot add a product to the cart
* **Symptom**: Clicking on a product in the grid does not add it to the POS cart, or typing/scanning the barcode shows an "Out of Stock" or "Blocked" alert.
* **Resolutions**:
  1. **Check Inventory**: Verify if the product has a quantity greater than `0` in your assigned Shop Warehouse. If physical items are on hand but not in the system, a supervisor must record a **Stock Adjustment** or approve a pending **Warehouse Transfer**.
  2. **Verify Pricing**: Ensure the product has an active **Selling Price** defined in the Product List. Products with a selling price of `0.00` are blocked from POS checkouts to prevent errors.
  3. **Scan Check**: Clean the scanner lens and ensure the cursor is active inside the barcode search field before scanning.

### Problem: Thermal receipt is not printing
* **Symptom**: POS sale confirms successfully, but the receipt printer remains silent.
* **Resolutions**:
  1. **Check Hardware**: Ensure the printer power light is green, paper roll is loaded correctly (thermal side facing up), and USB/Ethernet cabling is secure.
  2. **Clear Print Queue**: Open your computer's print manager, cancel all stuck document queues, and restart the printer.
  3. **Check Browser Dialog**: Verify if the browser's print dialog opened behind other windows. If so, select the correct receipt printer name and click **Print**.

## 2. System Settings & Configuration Lags

### Problem: Recently added brands, categories, or options are missing in dropdowns
* **Symptom**: You added a new category or product model in configuration, but POS or purchase forms do not show it.
* **Resolutions**:
  1. **Clear System Cache**: Click on your profile name at the top-right corner of the system tray and select **Clear Cache**.
  2. **Reload Web Page**: Hold `Ctrl + F5` (Windows) or `Cmd + Shift + R` (Mac) to force the browser to clear its cache and download the latest data.

## 3. Financial & Accounting Errors

### Problem: "Insufficient Funds" or "Operation Blocked" error during expense entry
* **Symptom**: Saving an expense or making a loan payment displays a validation block.
* **Resolutions**:
  1. **Verify Source Balance**: Check the balance of the Bank Account or Cash Drawer selected under the **Paid From** field. The system blocks expenses that exceed the available ledger balance.
  2. **Record Income / Transfers**: If the physical cash is in the drawer, ensure all recent customer dues collected or cash deposits have been logged to update the system ledger balance.

---

## 4. Workflows & Approvals

### Problem: Completed warehouse transfer is not updating destination stock
* **Symptom**: Stock has been dispatched from Warehouse A, but Shop B shows no stock increase.
* **Resolutions**:
  1. **Pending Acceptance**: The transfer is in 'Transit' status. The receiving branch manager must log in, navigate to Inventory Management → **Requisition Received**, select the pending shipment, verify counts, and click **Accept**.
  2. **Approval Hold**: Check if the transfer exceeds your store limit. If so, it will appear in the **Approval Inbox** and requires administrator approval.

## Summary of Key System Alerts

| Alert Message | Trigger Cause | Resolution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| `Product has no barcode...` | Item scanned does not have a SKU barcode registered. | Go to Product Master and add a barcode number. |
| `Negative stock not allowed` | The item is out of stock, and negative sales are disabled. | Restock items or enable negative sales in Settings. |
| `Unapproved purchase order...` | You are trying to sell stock from a purchase order that is not approved. | Ask Checker to approve the purchase order in the Inbox. |
| `Role does not have permission...` | User clicked a link restricted by role settings. | Ask Administrator to update your Role Permissions. |
