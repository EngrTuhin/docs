# Product Release Notes

This page summarizes new features, user experience enhancements, and business rules adjustments made in recent system versions.

---

## Version 1.2.0 (June 2026)

### Key Improvements:

#### 1. Point of Sale POS Attribute Merging
* **What is it**: When selling products with multiple variations (like size, color, or style), the system now merges them under a single card on the POS screen. 
* **Business Benefit**: Cashiers can click a single item card, and a clean **Attribute Selection Modal** will appear to let them choose the specific variant. This reduces screen clutter by up to 80% and allows cashiers to process transactions faster.
* **Scan Support**: Scanning the barcode of a specific variant automatically bypasses the modal and adds that exact variant to the checkout cart immediately.

#### 2. Unified Printing Dispatcher
* **What is it**: We consolidated all printing templates—A4 customer invoices, delivery challans, Mushak compliance receipts, and thermal checkout receipts—into a centralized system.
* **Business Benefit**: Ensures consistent branding and logo alignment across all printed materials, with automatic printer scaling.

#### 3. Custom Warehouse Column Rules
* **What is it**: Administrators can now hide specific pricing metrics and technical details (like SKU, Model, Size, cost values) on stock grids for specific business units.
* **Business Benefit**: Streamlines staff interfaces, showing only the fields relevant to their daily operations.

## Version 1.1.0 (March 2026)

### Key Improvements:

#### 1. Import Costing & LC Module
* **What is it**: A new module that links letters of credit (LC) to purchase work orders, tracking port duties, freight charges, and agent commissions.
* **Business Benefit**: Calculates a precise **Landed Cost Factor** for imported products, helping managers calculate exact margins.

#### 2. Dual-Verification (Maker-Checker) for Purchases
* **What is it**: An optional workflow where staff entries (Maker) must be verified and approved by a supervisor (Checker) before inventory updates.
* **Business Benefit**: Reduces human entry errors, preventing typos from throwing off balance sheets.

#### 3. Used Products Log
* **What is it**: A tracking system for inventory items pulled from stock for internal showroom or administrative use.
* **Business Benefit**: Ensures stock reductions for internal usage are logged as operational costs rather than disappearing.

## Version 1.0.0 (January 2026)

### Core Launch:
* **Point of Sale (POS)**: Real-time checkout with barcode scan and split payment options.
* **Multi-Warehouse & Multi-Shop Routing**: Move goods between central depots and retail counters.
* **Customer & Supplier Directories**: Store contact histories, branch addresses, and rolling financial ledgers.
* **Expense & Banking Ledgers**: Log expenses against bank accounts and mobile money channels.
* **Role-Based Access Control**: Lock down pages based on user roles (Cashier, Accountant, Admin).
