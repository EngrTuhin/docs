# Getting Started

Welcome to **Koorri POS & Inventory System**.

This page will help you login and start using the system.

<Info>
You do not need to register. Your admin or support team will give you the login link, username/email, and password.
</Info>

---

# 1. Get Login Details

Before login, collect these details from your admin or support team:

- Login link
- Email or username
- Password

## Example Login

| Field | Example |
|---|---|
| Email / Username | `admin@koorri.com` |
| Password | `Demo@12345` |

<Warning>
This is only an example. Use the real login details given by your admin.
</Warning>

---

# 2. Open Login Page

Open the login link in your browser.

You will see the login form.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Email / Username" icon="user">
    Enter your email or username.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Password" icon="lock">
    Enter your password.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Remember Workspace" icon="check-circle">
    Use this only on your own device.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Access Dashboard" icon="arrow-right">
    Click this button to login.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
Do not use Remember Workspace on a public or shared computer.
</Warning>

---

# 3. Login

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter email or username">
    Type your email or username.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter password">
    Type your password carefully.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Access Dashboard">
    Click the button to enter the system.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

# 4. Dashboard

After login, you will see the **Dashboard**.

The dashboard shows a quick summary of your business.

You may see:

- Sales
- Purchases
- Stock
- Customer due
- Supplier due
- Reports

<Info>
Your dashboard may look different based on your user role.
</Info>

---

# 5. Left Menu

Use the left menu to open system features.

| Menu | Use |
|---|---|
| Dashboard | View business summary |
| Product Configuration | Add products and product setup |
| Sales Management | Create sales and collections |
| Business Contacts | Add customers and suppliers |
| Purchase Management | Manage purchases |
| Inventory Management | Manage stock |
| Finance | Manage money records |
| Reports & Analytics | View reports |
| Settings | Manage users and permissions |

<Info>
You may not see all menus. Your admin controls your access.
</Info>

---

# 6. First Setup

For a new system, follow this order:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up products">
    Add categories, units, brands, products, and barcodes.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add contacts">
    Add customers and suppliers.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set up warehouse or shop">
    Add your stock location.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add opening stock">
    Enter your current stock quantity.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start daily work">
    Start sales, purchases, payments, and reports.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Card title="Next: Product Configuration" href="/docs/user-guide/product-configuration" icon="arrow-right">
  Start by setting up your products.
</Card>

# Safety Tips

- Use your own account
- Do not share your password
- Logout after work
- Do not save password on public computers
- Contact admin if you need help
